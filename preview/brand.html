Foundations · Brand

Brand

Two marks (wordmark + lime kettlebell glyph), a recurring mascot named Bell, and a stroke-based icon set. The brand is small and consistent on purpose — the product earns warmth from microcopy and the mascot, not from decoration.

Logo

The kettlebell glyph + "Ekklo" wordmark always sit together in the top-left of the sidebar with a small caret to indicate the workspace switcher. The mark is a stylized lime kettlebell with two leaf-like cuts.

Primary lockup

Used on light surfaces. Wordmark in ink, mark in lime.

Ekklo

Inverse

For dark surfaces — empty-state cards, the trial banner, the future mobile splash.

Ekklo

Mascot — meet Bell

Bell is a smiling green kettlebell. He shows up only in onboarding and empty states — never inside dense data. He's the warmth budget; everything else stays calm.

Greeting

Dashboard "Hello Fode 👋". Big, waving, off-duty.

Empty state

In long empty modules — Notifications, Automations.

Don't

No mascot in tables, no mascot inside graphs, no mascot at less than 56px.

🚫 not in dense data
use a stroke icon instead

Iconography

Lucide-style stroke icons at 1.5–1.7px weight, 18px in the sidebar and 16px inline. Icons inherit currentColor so they pick up nav state automatically.
