Foundations · Color · Proposals

Five color directions for Ekklo

Same architecture as today (one accent, one ink, one helper, neutrals everywhere else) — but tuned for different fitness-coach archetypes. Each direction is a complete, swappable system. The current Court palette is included as direction 1 so you can compare like-for-like.

Who uses Ekklo

Independent fitness & wellness coaches — personal trainers, nutritionists, online programs, boutique studios.

What they do here

Manage clients, sessions, programs, payments. The app is a dense operator console, not a workout app.

What the color does

Cut through density. Point at the next action on a busy screen. Stay calm everywhere else.

What to avoid

Generic "tech-startup" blues. Aggressive bodybuilder reds. Anything that fights the data-heavy tables and calendars.

How I'd read these

Each direction implies a slightly different Ekklo. Pick the brand stance first; the palette follows.

Next step. Tell me which 1–2 you want to see fully applied to the dashboard. I'll fork ui_kits/dashboard and produce a side-by-side mock so you can judge it under real density (calendar, accounting, exercise library) — not just on swatches.

  • Court — fresh, sporty, neutral on archetype. Already works. Keep if Ekklo wants the broadest appeal across coach types.
  • Sunrise — warm, hospitable, food-friendly. Best if Nutrition + lifestyle photography is central.
  • Pulse — high-intensity, polarizing. Best for HIIT / strength / boxing-leaning brands. Will alienate yoga-side coaches.
  • Trail — calm, longevity, holistic. Best for wellness / outdoor / mature audience. Quietest accent.
  • Studio — premium, business-first, sport-agnostic. Best if "serious tool for pros" beats "playful sports brand".
  • Forge — green ink + orange action, your stated recipe. Confident, grounded, broadest fit across coach types. A safe-but-distinctive default.
  • Iron — black + red, gym & intense workouts. Highest aggression. Best for strength / combat / HYROX brands; will alienate wellness side.
  • Meadow — green + soft blue, wellness & lifestyle. Calmest of the set. Best for nutrition, yoga, recovery, longevity coaches.
  • Neon — dark mode + neon green, modern premium. Sleek and techy. Best if Ekklo wants to read as performance tooling (Whoop / Strava-pro vibe).
  • Hearth — terracotta + cream + sage. Bridges Trail × Sunrise × Forge. Warm, grounded, broad appeal — strong default if you want one coherent recipe across coach types.
  • Citrus — mango + coffee + jade. Bridges Court × Sunrise. Keeps Court's "single bright moment" pattern but trades highlighter-lime for a softer, friendlier mango.
  • Orbit — dark charcoal + safety orange + green deltas. From your reference. Performance-coded, financial-console feel; reads as "tool for serious athletes" — strong opinion.
  • Atlas — neutral-first, ink CTA, spectrum priority chips. From your reference. The opposite of Court: no brand color owns the screen, hierarchy comes from typography and chip-color.
